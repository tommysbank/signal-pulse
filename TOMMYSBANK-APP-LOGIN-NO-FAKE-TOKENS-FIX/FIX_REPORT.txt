TOMMYSBANK APP FIX - 2026-07-15

What changed:
- Signals from Telegram are no longer published or pushed immediately just because a contract address was detected.
- A signal is now kept hidden until Dexscreener confirms a real market pair.
- If no pair is confirmed within the confirmation window, the pending signal is rejected and removed.
- Old cached/persisted unconfirmed signals are filtered out so fake tokens do not reappear after a restart.
- The external embedded live-chart section has been removed from the HTML and blocked from old cached DOM.
- Users should use the "Open chart" button for the external market chart.
- The login/access screen now avoids the repeated double-loading effect when a recent valid member session exists.
- Service worker/cache version was bumped so mobile installs fetch the updated files.

Render environment recommendation:
- STARTUP_CATCHUP_MINUTES=0
- MAX_SIGNAL_AGE_MINUTES=6
- PAIR_CONFIRMATION_MINUTES=8
- PRICE_INTERVAL_MS=4000
- NODE_OPTIONS=--max-old-space-size=256

Important:
- Upload these files to the Render app repository root, not to the Netlify website repository.
- After deploy, test from https://app.tommysignals.com and reinstall/refresh the home-screen app if the phone still shows old content.
